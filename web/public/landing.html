How It Works

From Booking to Celebration in Four Steps

We handle the complexity so you don't have to. Every quest is turnkey — just show up and explore.

We Design Your Scenario

Tell us the city, group size, and vibe you're after. We craft a custom route through the best cultural landmarks, with challenges tailored to your team.

Teams Get Their Codes

Each team receives a unique QR code or join link. No app downloads, no accounts — just scan and go from any smartphone browser.

Walk, Explore, Solve

Teams navigate the city at their own pace. At each landmark they unlock clues, answer questions, and earn points. A live leaderboard keeps the competitive spirit alive.

Celebrate Together

After the quest, gather to compare stories, review the scoreboard, and recognise the winning team. The shared adventure gives everyone something real to talk about.
